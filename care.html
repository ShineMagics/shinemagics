Crochet Care Protocol

Washing

Hand Wash Only:

Submerge in cool water using mild detergent. Avoid aggressive scrubbing to prevent fiber friction and pilling.


Machine Caution:

If necessary, use a mesh laundry bag on a delicate/wool cycle with cold water.


Drying

No Wringing:

Do not twist or wring. Press the item between two clean towels to remove excess moisture.


Flat Dry:

Reshape the item on a flat, ventilated surface away from direct sunlight to prevent stretching and color fading.


Storage and Maintenance

Fold, Don't Hang:

Hanging causes gravity to stretch the stitches, permanently altering the garment's shape. Store flat in a cool, dry place.


Pilling Management:

Remove surface fuzz manually or with a fabric shaver. Do not pull loose threads; weave them back into the fabric using a crochet hook or tapestry needle.


Amigurumi Specifics:

Spot clean plushies and amigurumi with a damp cloth to protect internal stuffing and safety eyes..
